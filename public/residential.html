Residential Services

From new construction to remodeling, we have what it takes to get your residential project done right, on time and on budget. Our dedicated, knowledgeable electricians work with every homeowner to ensure that their home is wired with safe, efficient electricity. Whether you need electrical services for your new home or your existing home, you can rely on us for high-quality services at reasonable prices. We offer a 1-year warranty on our residential electrical work.

We provide an extensive range of residential electrical services, including the following:

  • Troubleshoot and repair ceiling fans
  • Lighting work
  • Complete rewiring and wiring
  • Underground wiring
  • Exterior and interior electrical work
  • Surge protections
  • Motor services
  • Code corrections
  • Panel updates, repairs and installations
