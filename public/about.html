About Us

Hobson Electric has been Montgomery County's most trusted provider of electrical services for nearly two decades. Our customers know that they can rely on us to deliver efficient, quality workmanship on every project. We have done work for hundreds of homeowners and businesses throughout Montgomery County and the surrounding area. We have won The Paper of Montgomery County's Reader's Choice Award for Best Electrical Services Provider multiple times. Montgomery County residents and people throughout West Central Indiana know they can turn to us for high-quality, efficient electrical services.

Our owner and president, Daryl Hobson, has been a lifelong resident of West Central Indiana, and is an active member of the Crawfordsville community. Daryl is a member of Rock Point Baptist Church and volunteers his time working with the Celebrate Recovery program ministering to jail inmates dealing with addiction issues as well as serving as an assistant coach for Crawfordsville High School's baseball team.
